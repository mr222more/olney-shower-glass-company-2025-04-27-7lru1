About Us

Founded in 1995, Olney Shower Glass Company began with a vision to revolutionize bathroom design through innovative glass solutions. What started as a small family business has grown into a leading provider of premium shower enclosures, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the region.

Our mission is to combine cutting-edge technology with exceptional craftsmanship to create shower solutions that enhance both the functionality and aesthetics of modern bathrooms. We pride ourselves on our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, ensuring every installation exceeds expectations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does installation typically take?

Most installations are completed within 4-6 hours. Our professional team ensures minimal disruption to your daily routine while maintaining the highest quality standards.

What warranty do you offer?

We provide a comprehensive 10-year warranty on all glass and hardware components, covering manufacturing defects and mechanical issues.

How do I maintain the glass shower door?

Our EasyClean coating significantly reduces maintenance needs. A simple weekly wipe-down with a soft cloth and mild cleaner is typically sufficient to maintain the pristine appearance.

Can you customize the size and style?

Yes, we offer fully customizable solutions to fit any bathroom size and style preference. Our team will measure and design a solution specifically for your space.

What is the cost range for installation?

Prices vary based on size, style, and customization options. We offer competitive pricing starting at $1,200, with financing options available.
